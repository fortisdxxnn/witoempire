About
The company founded by Kian, was established in the year 2020 at the parish of Saint. Andrew. At the perception that the pandemic had wrought to the public, which is that the privation of food is a potent threat to civilization. This detriment propelled Kian along with a group of determined workers to embody the role of a benefactor for the betterment of the country amidst the destructive crisis. We build the Witter, Igwebuike, Thomas and O’Connor’s Empire Supermarket and convene to confer the profound name to it called WITO’s Empire grocery. The name symbolically indicates an empire that raises aloft the insurance of a starvation-free society. Our motivation to thrive for the profit, which is the goal of the abundance of food being pervaded among Jamaicans, is professing that “We provide order and consolation in distress and chaos.” The services we convey enable easy access for every individual to acquire the variety of foods we offer. We have branches across the country with a robust delivery service to distributed foods of anyone’s desire. At every branch strict safety protocols are observed for the promotion of our safety against the pandemic’s malign devices. The categories of products are the following – beverage, canned foods, condiments, produce, snacks, vegetables, refrigerated food, kitchen items, frozen food, meats, etc. We are open unless the emergence of a curfew, from Monday to Saturday on 8:00am to 10:00pm; for Sundays it’s 9:00am to 6:00pm.
